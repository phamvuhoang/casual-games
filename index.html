Casual Games Index

Each panel below embeds one of the AR experiences in this repository. Make sure to allow camera permissions when prompted and keep your hands visible in the webcam view. Pinch, poke, cross, and other gestures trigger the special moves described inside each title.

Starbridge Express

Pinch to draw rails on your desk and watch them appear in the isometric relay yard. Confirm each line to sync and let the tiny trains loop between worlds.

Pinch to draw, confirm to sync, reset to clear. Open in new tab

AR Gesture Shooter

Track the pistol pose, aim with your index finger, and pinch with the thumb to fire against hovering targets. Floating synth cues and a neon HUD keep you informed of every successful shot.

Pinch to shoot, raise your hand to unlock the crosshair. Open in new tab

AR Ninja: Dual Blade

Slice neon fruit orbs with both hands, rack up combos, trigger slow-motion with a pinch, and unleash the fist-powered ultimate. Choose classic or survival modes while keeping bombs at bay.

AR Meteor Blaster

A neon defense grid tracks your finger-gun pose to aim lasers at swarms of meteors, bombs, and power-ups while a shield, score, and ultimate meter animate the rush. Hold the trigger to overcharge, cross arms to unleash a NOVA blast, and flash an open palm to pause the mission as the swarm intensifies.

Finger gun to aim, thumb tap to fire, cross arms for the ultimate nova burst. Open in new tab

AR Balloon Pop Carnival

Race a 60-second timer by poking balloons with your index finger or pinching to hurl darts, stacking combos for a mega meter and festive pop animations. Glittering HUD readouts keep score, combo streaks, and time visible while the carnival soundtrack pulses in the background.

Index poke to pop, pinch to throw darts, and keep the combos alive before time runs out. Open in new tab

AR Anime Skills

Switch between Spirit Gun and Kamehameha drills while the mirror camera feeds your real-time pose; index-gun shots trigger cyan beams, and charging both wrists together releases a sweeping blue energy wave. Score boosts reset the timer and flip skills automatically so you can keep stacking hits while the HUD tracks every success.
